#GOVERNMENT SCHEMES

ABOUT

Government implements a variety of schemes for the welfare of citizens. But many people are not availing it due to lack of proper information about the schemes, eligibility criteria, where and how to utilize it. The main aim of this application is to collect accurate information of all government schemes and make the information available to the users. .



PERCEIVE INDIA is the one stop solution for the search and discovery of government schemes relevant to the user.
